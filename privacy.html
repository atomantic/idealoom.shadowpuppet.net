Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 28, 2026

Data Storage

Idea Loom stores all your data locally on your device using UserDefaults. Your idea lists, drafts, favorites, streaks, and settings never leave your device unless you explicitly choose to export or share them.

Data We Do Not Collect

Idea Loom does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or fingerprints
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Advertising identifiers
  • The content of your ideas or lists

In-App Purchases

Idea Loom offers optional prompt packs via Apple's In-App Purchase system. All payment processing is handled entirely by Apple. We do not receive, store, or have access to your payment information, credit card details, or Apple ID. For information about how Apple handles purchase data, please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy.

Third-Party Services

Idea Loom does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. There are no SDKs, pixels, or external scripts in the app.

No Account Required

Idea Loom does not require any account creation, login, or authentication.

Export & Sharing

When you export an idea list to Apple Notes or share it, the data is handled through Apple's standard sharing mechanisms. We do not intercept, store, or transmit exported data.

Home Screen Widget

Idea Loom includes a Home Screen widget that displays your daily prompt and streak. Widget data is shared between the app and widget using an App Group container stored locally on your device. No data is transmitted externally.

Children's Privacy

Idea Loom does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app contains no social features, user-generated content sharing, or external communication mechanisms.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by uninstalling the app. All data stored in UserDefaults and the App Group container is removed when the app is deleted.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at idealoom@shadowpuppet.net.
